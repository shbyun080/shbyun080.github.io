I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the University of Cambridge, working with Prof. Rafal Mantiuk. I currently work as a Research Scientist at LG Electronics Silicon Valley Emerging Technology Lab, CTO Office.

Previously, I received my M.S. in Computer Science (AI) at University of Southern California and B.S. in Computer Science & Engineering at University of California, Irvine. At UCI, I worked closely with Prof. Aditi Majumder on medical AI.

Research

My research focuses on Graphics (e.g., Neural Rendering), Computer Vision (e.g., Visual Representation, VLM), and Efficient AI (e.g., Adaptive Token, LLM Compression). My recent works explore:

  • Representation in vision-language models
  • Compression and acceleration of LLM
  • Visual analytical systems
